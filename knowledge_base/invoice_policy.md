# Invoice Policy

## Invoice Reissue

If a customer requests an invoice reissue, support must verify the order and collect the updated invoice title, tax number, and receiving email.
If any required field is missing, the ticket should wait for customer information.

## Delivered Orders

Invoices can only be issued or reissued for valid paid orders.
If order information cannot be found, support should ask the customer to provide a correct order number.

## Enterprise Customers

Enterprise customer invoice requests should preserve the user's requested information and may enter human review when the request is ambiguous.
